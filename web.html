Tips & Tools for Healthy Living

Meditation Exercises

Try these simple breathing and relaxation exercises to reduce stress and promote calmness:

  • Find a quiet place and sit comfortably.
  • Breathe deeply through your nose for 4 seconds.
  • Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
  • Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds.
  • Repeat this 5-10 times, focusing on your breath.

Light Exercises

Stay active with these gentle exercises that promote mobility and circulation:

  • Stretch your arms and legs for 5 minutes every morning.
  • Take a brisk 10-minute walk around your campus or neighborhood.
  • Try simple yoga poses like child's pose or seated twists.

Study-Life Balance Tips

Keep a healthy balance between your studies and personal life:

  • Set specific study hours and stick to them.
  • Take regular breaks to rest and refresh your mind.
  • Engage in hobbies and social activities.
